About Smile Boutique Inc

A calm space for real-life smiles.

Smile Boutique Inc is a dedicated smile and dental hygiene studio in Reyno Ridge, Witbank. Led by LDN Motshana, we focus on preventative care, gentle deep cleaning, cosmetic whitening and curated dental hygiene products that you can trust.

  • Friendly, personalised care in a relaxed, boutique-style setting.
  • Clear explanations before every treatment, no surprises.
  • Treatment plans tailored to your lifestyle, habits and goals.
  • Education-first approach so you know how to maintain your results at home.
New Patients

Ask about our Smile Assessment Visit – a focused appointment to assess your gums, teeth shade and home-care routine before recommending any whitening or treatment.

Your first visit

During your first visit we take time to understand what you’d like to improve, assess the health of your teeth and gums, and then discuss realistic options for whitening and long-term maintenance.

Average appointment: 45 – 60 minutes

Who we’re ideal for

People who want a cleaner, brighter, healthier smile, without looking “overdone”. Perfect for graduations, weddings, professional headshots or simply feeling more confident every day.

Treatments

What we can help you with

We combine in-chair treatments with carefully selected at-home products to help you maintain a bright, healthy smile between visits.

Professional Dental Cleaning

Gentle yet thorough scaling and polishing to remove plaque and surface stains, leaving your teeth feeling smooth and fresh.

Ideal every 6 months.

Teeth Whitening

In-chair whitening tailored to your tooth shade and sensitivity, aiming for a natural-looking brightness rather than an artificial “glow in the dark” white.

Consultation required before treatment.

Smile Refresh Packages

Combine cleaning, whitening and take-home care into one convenient visit designed around your calendar and upcoming events.

Home-Care Coaching

One-on-one guidance on brushing, flossing and product choices based on your actual mouth, not generic advice from the internet.

In-house Hygiene Products

Curated products selected for effectiveness and safety, including options for sensitive teeth and gums.

Maintenance Visits

Regular check-ins to keep your smile on track, adjust products as needed and keep small issues from becoming big ones.
